WooCommerce

What is WooCommerce?

WooCommerce is an open-source e-commerce plugin for WordPress that transforms any website into a powerful online store. Key features include:

  • Product Management: List physical/digital products, variants, and subscriptions.
  • Payment Gateways: Integrate M-Pesa, Flutterwave, PayPal, and Stripe.
  • Shipping & Tax: Auto-calculate rates for local/global sales.
  • Extensions: 50,000+ plugins for memberships, bookings, and more.

Who is WooCommerce For?

1. WordPress Users

Best for: Businesses already using WordPress who want to add e-commerce functionality.

Example: A Kenyan blogger monetizes their site by selling eBooks via WooCommerce.

2. Budget-Conscious Businesses

Best for: Startups/SMEs needing a low-cost (but powerful) online store.

Example: A Nigerian artisan sells handmade crafts with $0 upfront platform fees.

3. Businesses Needing Customization

Best for: Stores requiring unique designs or specialized features.

Example: A South African wine farm builds a membership-based wine club.

4. B2B & Wholesale Sellers

Best for: Companies needing quote systems, bulk pricing, or approval workflows.

Example: A Tanzanian hardware supplier automates bulk orders for contractors.
