What is Odoo ERP?

Odoo is an open-source business management platform with integrated apps for:

  • Sales & CRM (manage leads, orders, customers)
  • Inventory & Warehouse (real-time stock tracking)
  • Accounting & Invoicing (automated financial workflows)
  • Manufacturing & Purchasing (optimize supply chains)
  • HR & Payroll (employee management)
