What Is E-Commerce?

E-commerce (electronic commerce) is the buying and selling of goods/services online. It includes:

  • Online stores (Shopify, WooCommerce, Jumia)
  • Marketplaces (Amazon, eBay, Kilimall)
  • Subscription services (monthly boxes, SaaS)
  • Digital products (e-books, courses, software)

5 Reasons Your Business Needs E-Commerce

1. Reach More Customers 24/7

  • Physical stores are limited by location and hours.
  • Online stores sell to customers across Kenya, Africa, and globally.
  • Example: A Nairobi handmade jewelry brand now exports to Europe via Shopify.

2. Lower Operating Costs

  • No need for expensive rent, utilities, or in-store staff.
  • Example: A Nakuru bakery cuts costs by taking 50% of orders online.

3. Automate Sales & Inventory

  • Sync orders, stock, and payments automatically.
  • Example: A Kampala fashion store uses Odoo ERP to prevent overselling.

4. Data-Driven Growth

  • Track best-selling products, customer behavior, and marketing ROI.
  • Example: A Lagos electronics shop doubles sales by analyzing trends.

5. Scale Faster

  • Easily add new products, markets, or sales channels (WhatsApp, Instagram).
  • Example: A Rwandan coffee brand expands to 10 countries via WooCommerce.
